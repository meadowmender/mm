FAQs

Here are some Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs):


Q1: What is MeadowMender ?

A. MeadowMender provides Land Maintenance services and Subsistence Agriculture Solutions to land owners from the state of Kerala. Our services are best suited for those who are unable to tend to their property themselves due to time constraints or because of being geographically located remotely, away from their property.


Q2: What services are covered under the Care Package ?

A. We provide a wide range of services for you to take care of that patch you have.

  1. Quarterly visits to your property.
  2. General Health Assessment of Trees and existing Crops. The assessment report will be published to you every quarter along with pictures of your plot.
  3. Crops/Trees will be inspected clearly for health, pests, weeds etc. and this will be reported back to you. We will also highlight fertilization and manuring needs.
  4. We will communicate about produce-gathering opportunities as the season approaches, for mangoes, jack fruit, coconuts, etc.
  5. Any Unauthorized Activities identified at your site during our visits will be informed to you/ your local contact via email or by telephone depending on the urgency – so that you can take further action as needed.
  6. Access to our On-Demand Services. You may choose to avail our On-Demand Services at any point in time as part of the care package. Read more about On-Demand services here.


Q3: Can I pay my land tax through your organization ?

A. Yes, we can assist you will paying your land tax as part of our On-Demand service.


Q4: How are On-Demand Services Charged ?

A. On-Demand Services are charged on actuals + a nominal service fee. Once you choose to avail one of our On Demand Services we will prepare a quote in line with current competitive market pricing. Once you approve the quote – We will get right to work! When the work is complete, we will send you a post implementation report with pictures of the work performed.


Q5: Can I request for an On-Demand Service without going in for a Care Package ?

A. Unfortunately we are unable to provide an On Demand Service to you without a valid Care Package.


Q6: My Question is not answered on this page. How can I get my question answered ?

A. Our sincere apologies for not being able to have answered your query. Please email us at support@meadowmender.com and we will get back to you right away.
