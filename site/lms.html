What we do!

MeadowMender provides land maintenance services and subsistence agriculture solutions to land owners with property in the state of Kerala. Our services are best suited for those who are unable to tend to their property themselves due to time constraints or because of being located remotely, away from their property.

What does Land Maintenance comprise of ?

Land Maintenance includes a wide variety of activities: from visiting your patch of land periodically and assessing the health of crops and trees, to informing you about any damage to your land from natural causes or from unauthorized activities.

We can help you with constructing protective measures such as compound walls and fencing, if needed. We can also help you in setting up produce storage facilities or protective measures against rain or flooding through land elevation.
