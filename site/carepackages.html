Care Packages

Our care packages provide the following assured services to you.

  1. Quarterly visits to your property.
  2. General Health Assessment of Trees and existing Crops. The assessment report will be published to you every quarter along with pictures of your plot.
  3. Crops/Trees will be inspected clearly for health, pests, weeds etc. and this will be reported back to you. We will also highlight fertilization and manuring needs.
  4. We will communicate about produce-gathering opportunities as the season approaches, for mangoes, jack fruit, coconuts, etc.
  5. Any Unauthorized Activities identified at your site during our visits will be informed to you/ your local contact via email or by telephone depending on the urgency – so that you can take further action as needed.
  6. Access to our On-Demand Services. You may choose to avail our On-Demand Services at any point in time as part of the care package. Read more about on demand services here.
