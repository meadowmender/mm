Services

We will be your feet on the ground. Our field experts will visit your property on a periodic basis (quarterly or on-demand) to keep a close watch on your land and your crops. The following services are assured as part of our care package:

  1. General health assessment of trees and existing crops. The assessment report will be sent to you periodically, along with pictures of your plot via email.
  2. If crops like plantain, pineapple, yam, colocasia, etc, have been cultivated, these will be inspected for pests and weeds. This information will be reported to you as well. We can also recommend fertilization and manuring needs.
  3. We will communicate about produce gathering opportunities as the season approaches.
  4. Any Unauthorized Activities identified at your site during our visits will be informed to you/your local contact via email or by telephone depending on the urgency – so that you can take further action as needed.
  5. Availing our care packages will also enable access to our On Demand Services.

And the following are some of our On Demand Services*:

  • Perform application of Fertilizer and Pesticides.
  • Checking the Property and Collecting fallen Coconuts / Arracknuts, etc and storing them onsite. We can also forward the produce to your local contact. Additionally, you may also avail the option to sell the produce and make a profit.
  • When the season arrives we can manage produce gathering / transportation and Sale of your product with your consent.
  • Construction of protective measures such as compound wall/fencing and produce storage facilities.
  • Set up pre-monsoon protective measures such as mud banks and Rainwater Harvesting solutions.
  • Soil tilling and weed removal. This improves the fertility of the soil and, thereby, improves your produce.
  • Any other tasks can also be carried out, based on terms mutually agreed upon.

*Based on actuals plus a nominal service charge.
