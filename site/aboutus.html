About Us

Our community consists of a group of individuals who has been practicing small-scale agriculture for well over 40 years in the state of Kerala. We are passionate about the work we do and we are passionate about our home state. We know the land and what it takes to maintain it

The times are changing

The last few decades have brought a lot of changes to the demography of Kerala. Priorities have changed and many people are unable to find the time to pursue our traditional culture of subsistence agriculture – concentrating more on full-time occupation outside the state and now, increasingly, out of the country. We understand that given an opportunity, most people would like to tend to their land — keep it weed free, keep an eye on the coconut trees, fertilize the crops when needed and perhaps even construct a compound wall, but can’t find the time to do so.

Meadowmender - Your feet on the ground!

Our primary aim is to assist you by being your hands and feet on the ground. Tending to and taking care of your asset or property—through timely visits to your plot, checking your grove for infestation by pests and insects, store and manage any produce as per your request. We will also periodically send you reports with pictures and a write up about your land and how best you can look to utilize it further.


MeadowMender is a subsidiary of the Ajax Construction Company which has been operating in the state of Kerala for well over 25 years. K. G. Praphulla Kumar, who graduated as an engineer from CET – Trivandrum, is the Chairperson of the Board.

Mr. K. G. Praphulla Kumar himself is a subsistence agriculturist. MeadowMender is further supported by agricultural experts and professors in the field of Botany and Dendrology.

And last but not the least, we are passionate about our planet. Our methods and practices revolve around the use of fertilizer and pesticide that are non-toxic to our environment and the life that it sustains.
