How it works!

Here is how meadowmender works!



Step 1: Sign up on MeadowMender. As part of this process we will record – your profile information and the location of your property. You will also choose a package. Check them out here.



Step 2: Our Service Team will get in touch with you to plan our first visit to your property. A date will be identified on which our field representative will visit your property.



Step 3: The Field Representative will visit your land and perform a preliminary assessment of your property. As part of the first assessment, we will also provide an inventory of major crops and trees present on your property.



Step 4: Our review team will then prepare a report for you. We will ensure that all significant and pertinent information with repect to your property are highlighted in the report.



Step 5: The report is then sent to you. You can review the information and reach out to us if you would like us to take any further action based on the report.

A fresh report will be sent to you as part of every visitation cycle. During our visits, in case of any untoward issues identified, these will be highlighted in the report. In case the issue warrants immediate communication, we will reach out to you via telephone.


The report will also highlight any produce gathering opportunities, manuring and fertilization needs as well. You can then decide on what action to take as per your wish. We can cater to these requests as part of our on demand services.


Please do note that, on demand services are available to you at any point in time and the activity can be carried out, outside the standard visitation cycles.
